Who are We

Maple View is a Bible based, Holy Spirit led church family.

When you first arrive at Maple View, you’ll be greeted at the front door by one of our Sunday morning greeters. Grab a snack and a cup of coffee from our coffee table as you head up to the sanctuary. If you have a child 3 years old or younger, feel free to take them down to the nursery at any point during our Sunday morning service. If you have kids 4 through 13 years old, there’s Children’s Church (ages 4-9) and Junior Sunday School (ages 9-13) following the worship time. Our worship is contemporary with modern songs and sometimes a few classic hymns thrown in. Feel free to dress in what makes you comfortable. We have people who dress up on Sunday mornings to people who wear jeans.

Most of all, we would just like you to visit and connect (or reconnect) with God and his church family.

Nursery

Nursery is available on Sunday mornings during our church service. Nursery is for children 0-3 years old and is located in the church basement. All nursery volunteers must take a criminal background check.


Children's Church

Children’s Church is on Sunday mornings for ages 4-9 years old. Dismissal is after our time of worship. Your child will learn Bible stories and principles. All Children Church teachers go through a criminal background check.

Adult Sunday School

Join us on Sunday mornings at 9:15am


Traditional Bible Study– in the Conference Room, studying the Standard Lesson Quarterlies.
